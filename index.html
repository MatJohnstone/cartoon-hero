Edna Mode

About Edna Mode

Edna Marie Mode is a fashion-designer & inventor, known for crafting the costumes of the world's best-loved superheroes.

Edna and her iconic glasses

Edna's Work

  • Mode designed every Incredible's costume, including Elastagirl & Mr Incredible.
  • Her work during the 'Golden Age of Superheroes' is now considered legendary.
  • Frozone's costume is perhaps her best-known creation.
